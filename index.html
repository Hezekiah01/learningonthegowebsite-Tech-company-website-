About LEARNING ON THE GO

Our mission is to bridge Africa's digital skills gap by training 10 million youths over the next decade. Since 2018, we've empowered thousands through technology, vocational, and business startup programs.

Partnering with Covenant University, we deliver world-class training in AI, Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, and more. Our alumni now lead impactful startups and remote careers across Fintech, Edtech, and emerging tech fields.
